How to play Minecraft 

History about the Game

In 2009, Minecraft was created by Markus Persson, also known as Notch and was originally called Cave Game. This game was different than other online games where you create things because instead of simply creating structures, you also have to gather resources to build those structures. This mix was unusual because most games at the time usually only had the player gather resources and not build or to build but not have to gather resources. On top of that, Minecraft was also a survival game. You have to survive hostile mobs and -can explore the vast world. You can also use your resources to craft new materials that you can use to create structures, survive longer, and craft more materials.

How to play the game

As mentioned above, Minecraft is a Vast game. Many consider the objective to be killing the ender dragon. But, even that can be done in many different ways. Some players can choose to gather the bare minimum in resources in order to defeat the boss, while others may want to take their time to be extra prepared while also completing unrelated side quests. Unlike other games, the game does not end after killing the boss. Players can choose to extend their world by building whaever they want. In fact, some players have worlds ovr 10 years old!                                       

Because Minecraft is such a vast game, this guide will be focusing on an important aspect of it, which is Mining. Mining is very important (Its in the name) because it provides players with many of the resources needed to advance in the game. Materials found underground can help craft protection, tools, and provide building resources.                                       

How to prepare for your mining session 

  1. First, Make sure you have lots of food. Mining uses up hunger and it is very time consuming/ inefficent to come back up to the surface for more food. You also don't want to risk dying to any mobs when you have no source of regeneration.                           
  2. Next, you want to make sure that you have multiple iron+ level pickaxes. They must be atleast iron level to make sure you are eligible to find any diamonds you may find, and make sure you have multiple to avoid having to come up to surface.                 
  3. Lastly, make sure to have torches. These are important to light up the areas you are mining in to allow better visibility.
